# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users
Founders, brand owners, and product teams looking for a distinctive, high-performance digital presence. They value technical precision and "loud" visual impact over safe, generic aesthetics.

## Product Purpose
Webyfrog exists to deliver "Digital Brutality"—high-performance, high-impact web systems that command attention. Success is defined by a visitors' immediate recognition of the brand's technical authority and bold POV.

## Positioning
Precision-engineered digital agency. We build high-performance systems for brands that value impact over aesthetics.

## Conversion & proof
- Primary and secondary CTA: "Start a Project" (Primary), "View Portfolio" (Secondary).
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "Webyfrog: Digital Brutality."
- Belief ladder:
  1. Generic web design is failing to capture attention.
  2. Performance and precision are the foundations of impact.
  3. Webyfrog's Neo-Brutalist approach is the technical antidote to the SaaS-cream monoculture.
- Proof on hand: Portfolio showcase of high-performance sites (represented in `/portfolio`).

## Brand Personality
Bold, high-performance, precision-engineered, unapologetic.

## Anti-references
SaaS-cream palettes (beige/muted), timid typography, generic card-grid layouts, safe "modern" aesthetics.

## Design Principles
1. **Impact over Aesthetics**: Prioritize visual punch and technical clarity.
2. **Precision Engineering**: Every detail (spacing, motion, code) must feel calibrated.
3. **Neo-Brutalist Honesty**: Use raw materials (hard shadows, strokes, mono fonts) with high intent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. Ensure that despite high-contrast Neo-Brutalist styling, readability and keyboard navigation remain excellent.
